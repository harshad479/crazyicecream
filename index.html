Our Story

CRAZY ICE-CREAM was established by visionary founder director Mr. Mahesh Patil and Mr. Santaji Kadam in the year 1999 at Vasantdada Industrial Estate, Sangli, Maharashtra. The company has got excel command over manufacturing and supplying of medium fat ice-cream. Within short time span the company has been scaling new heights of success in all its business endeavors. The company has acquired a remarkable benchmark in ice-cream manufacturers and suppliers based in domestic market and south-westen Maharashtra.

CRAZY ICE-CREAM established a state-of-the-art infrastructure facility that is spread in vast area and is backed with technologically advanced tools and machinery required for production of the best quality ice-cream. CRAZY ICE-CREAM's professional production facility plays a significant role in meeting all kinds of small and bulk requirements of the clients.

CRAZY ICE-CREAM is committed to serve superior quality products to all our valuable consumers. we conduct various stringent quality checks from the formulation to the final dispatch of product in the market. These quality checks are conducted under the professionals who examine all products maintaining their highest quality and durability which will satisfy our consumers needs.

CRAZY ICE-CREAM developed a big business network spreading across Maharashtra. Our strong network is well-supported with proper modes of transportation ensuring timely delivery of the products to the suppliers and clients. Our business network and dedicated hard-work helping us building strong and long lasting relationships with the our esteemed distributors, suppliers and clients.
